### Core Requirements
  GERM 204 | Essential German for Advanced Proficiency: Vielfalt in Practice H | 3hrs
  GERM 301 | Advanced Spoken German: Vielfalt in Media | 3hrs
  GERM 302 | Advanced Written German: Vielfalt in Analytic and Creative Writing | 3hrs
RULE: Two electives courses. Students can choose two German-language classes, or two English-language classes, or one German-language and one English-language class from the following options: 6
RULE: A. Applied German courses (taught in German): GERM 303 , GERM 304 , GERM 305 , GERM 306 , GERM 493
RULE: B. Themes courses (taught in English) numbered GERM 210 - GERM 290 and GSLL 212 - GSLL 259 , GSLL 269 , GSLL 270 , GSLL 274, GSLL 278 , GSLL 280 , GSLL 282 , GSLL 284, GSLL 288 , GSLL 295
RULE: C. Histories courses (taught in English) numbered GERM 401 - GERM 492
RULE: D. Theories courses (taught in English) numbered GERM 550 - GERM 589
NOTE: Students who receive placement credit (PL) or By-Examination credit (BE) for GERM 204 and/or GERM 301 must substitute this credit with coursework (three credit hours each to replace GERM 204 and/or GERM 301 ) to complete the requirements for the major. The additional coursework must be German-language coursework numbered above the German-language course that students test out of. Students may not re-enroll in a course for which they have received PL or BE credit.
