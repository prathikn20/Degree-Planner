## Admission Requirements
RULE: Students must complete the following courses:
  CHEM 261 | Introduction to Organic Chemistry I H | 3hrs
  MATH 231 | Calculus of Functions of One Variable I H, F | 4hrs
RULE: Students must complete or be enrolled simultaneously in the following courses:
  CHEM 262 | Introduction to Organic Chemistry II H | 3hrs
RULE: It is also strongly advised that students have already completed the following courses:
  BIOL 240 | Cell Biology H | 3hrs
  BIOL 252 | Fundamentals of Human Anatomy and Physiology (or comparable course) H | 3hrs
