### Core Requirements
RULE: Two art history foundation courses from ARTH 100 to ARTH 200 6
RULE: Nine ARTH courses above 200, apportioned in the following way 1 27
RULE: At least one course from three of the four geographic areas (see course lists below)
RULE: At least one course from each of the three chronological periods (see course lists below)
RULE: At least three courses must be numbered above 399
  ARTH 391 | Undergraduate Research Seminar 2 | 3hrs
NOTE: A first-year seminar taught by an art history faculty member may substitute for one art history course numbered between 201 and 399.  Students may also substitute an ARTS (Studio Art) course for one art history course numbered between 201 and 399.
NOTE: It is strongly recommended that students take the Undergraduate Research Seminar no later than the spring of their junior year. The department schedules ARTH 391 as often as possible. If ARTH 391 has not been offered during a student's junior or senior year, the student may satisfy the requirement with an additional ARTH course numbered above 399 (in addition to the standard three required as part the major). Such exceptions must be approved by the Director of Undergraduate Studies.
