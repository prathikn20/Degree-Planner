NOTE: Please note that First Year Seminars (all numbered below 100) and courses with a second digit of nine ( PHIL 390 , PHIL 691H , PHIL 692H , etc.) automatically count toward the major core as electives. NOTE: Within the framework of the major, students may elect to take philosophy courses geared toward prelaw. Inquiries about prelaw advising may be sent to the department's director of undergraduate studies. Although we are not in a position to offer law career (including career prep) or LSAT advice, the director of undergraduate studies is available to provide academic advising and discuss prelaw course information.
