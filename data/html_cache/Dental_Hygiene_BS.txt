## Dental Hygiene Professional School Program
NOTE: DHYG 357 , DHYG 357L , and DHYG 495 will be taken in the summer semester between the first year and second year.
NOTE: In addition to the program requirements listed above, students must attain a final cumulative GPA of at least 2.0. For more information, please consult the degree requirements section of the catalog, and the Dental Hygiene Programs Academic Policy.

## Additional Information for the Dental Hygiene Programs
NOTE: Upon provisional acceptance into the dental hygiene program, all students must provide an acceptable criminal background check and provide documentation of required immunizations. See "Immunization Requirements" details in the Compliance section.
NOTE: One of the consequences of the delivery of health care is the possibility of contracting infectious disease such as tuberculosis, hepatitis, herpes, or HIV. To minimize this risk, the UNC Adams School of Dentistry has adopted an Infection Control Policy that requires the wearing of a clinical overgarment, disposable gloves, a mask, shield, protective cap and protective safety eye covering when performing oral examinations and dental hygiene services.
NOTE: Dental school charges are required, including instrument rental and other supplies needed for clinical care and the education of patients. Additional costs may occur for projects required in the curriculum.
