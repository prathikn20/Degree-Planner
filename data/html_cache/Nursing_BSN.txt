## Requirements of the Major

## A.B.S.N. and B.S.N. Options
NOTE: Students are admitted to the baccalaureate nursing program at the upper-division level. All lower-division courses must be completed before beginning nursing courses; see the admission criteria and prerequiste information in the Catalog .
NOTE: Students are subject to the requirements in place when they are admitted to the School of Nursing; consequently, the requirements described in this catalog particularly apply to students admitted to the School of Nursing during the 2025–2026 academic year and beyond. Students admitted in prior years will follow the plans of study in the catalog year in which they were admitted to the School of Nursing.
### Core Requirements
  NURS 301 | Building Effective Relationships in Healthcare: Carolina Core I | 3hrs
  NURS 302 | Research, Ethics, and Innovation: Carolina Core  II | 3hrs
  NURS 310 | Clinical Nursing Skills and Health Assessment I: On Campus Clinical I | 2hrs
  NURS 311 | Clinical Nursing Skills and Health Assessment II: On Campus Clinical II | 1hrs
  NURS 330 | Nursing Care of Adults I | 4hrs
  NURS 351 | Pathophysiology/Pharmacology Across the Lifespan I | 3hrs
  NURS 352 | Pathophysiology/Pharmacology Across the Lifespan II | 3hrs
  NURS 384 | Family Focused Genomic Health Care | 1hrs
  NURS 401 | Integrating Principles of Leadership, Quality and Safety, and Informatics into Nursing Practice | 3hrs
  NURS 402 | Foundations of Population Health and Global Health: Carolina Core IV | 2hrs
  NURS 410 | Clinical Nursing Skills and Health Assessment III: On Campus Clinical III | 1hrs
  NURS 411 | Clinical Nursing Skills and Health Assessment IV: On Campus Clinical IV | 1hrs
  NURS 430 | Nursing Care of Adults II | 5hrs
  NURS 481 | Mental Health Promotion and Psychiatric Care Across Populations and Settings | 5hrs
  NURS 482 | Reproductive Health and Nursing Care of the Childbearing Family | 5hrs
  NURS 483 | Family-Centered Nursing Care from Birth through Adolescence | 5hrs
  NURS 484 | Public Health Nursing in Community Settings | 4hrs
  NURS 697 | Capstone: Transitions in Care and Practice | 6hrs
  NURS--- | Required NURS elective 1 | 3hrs
NOTE: A total of 3 hours of NURS elective is required. This may be a single course, or multiple NURS courses that total at least 3 hours. NURS electives may be taken in any semester.
