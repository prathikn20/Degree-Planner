### Additional Requirements
RULE: Complete a 280-hour internship with a start-up or employer approved by the internship coordinator of the minor.
NOTE: ECON 57H , ECON 111 , ECON 125 , ECON 325 , ECON 327 , and ECON 393 cannot be counted toward an economics major.
NOTE: The minor in entrepreneurship anticipates adding additional track courses. For a complete listing of these courses contact the Minor in Entrepreneurship office. One track course must be taken for students to complete the minor. Students are able to take more than one track course for elective credit and hours toward graduation.
NOTE: It is also possible to complete the entrepreneurship minor through completing one economics course and then a one-semester Shuford Away program in Silicon Valley (California) or London. These are programs run jointly with Honors Carolina. To learn more, contact the Minor in Entrepreneurship office.
