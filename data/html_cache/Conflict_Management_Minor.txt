NOTE: BUSI 470 , BUSI 545 and BUSI 555 are 1.5 credit hour courses. Students who choose two of these courses (for a total of 3 hours) will need to select two additional courses (for a total of 9 hours) to fulfill this requirement. Conflict management minors will not receive priority registration for these courses. Minors will need to apply for these courses by completing the "Non-major Request Form."
